# Finding Beauty in Negative Space

In photography, what you *leave out* of the frame is often more important than what you put in.

## Tip 1: The Rule of Thirds
Don't put your subject in the middle. Let them breathe on the edges.

## Tip 2: High Contrast
Black and white photography is perfect for minimalism because it strips away the distraction of color.
